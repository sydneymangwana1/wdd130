We run rivers to relax

Raftolious is a specialized white water rafting tour operator on the Orange River in Drakensburg. We are the oldest river rafting company in South Africa. We have been running Orange River Rafting trips for more than 25 years, dating back as far as 1985 from the same original Bundi Camp location. We enjoy an exceptional record regarding safety and competence. We offer an unforgettable rowing adventure that restores the senses and stimulates the soul. Enjoy one of our excusion trips to the majestic, scenic and exhilarating Orange River! Our section we raft on runs through the scenic Richtersveld in KwaZulu-Natal. Feel free to call us for more information! Your Raftolious trip will not only include beautiful scenery, friendly guides and exciting rapids, we also include full catering for your whole trip from the arrival evening until the departure morning.

We run whitewater for the rush

Indulge in tranquil lazy drifts on the Orange River. We offer great paddling and rafting adventures. Our rafting excursions are available throughout the year and are open to everyone. Our packages vary from half day getaways to multi day river adventures. Join The River Rafters on one of our upcoming awesome rafting adventures. It’s exciting and stimulating. This will sure be a rafting adventure you will never forget! Our professional guides will look after your safety, provide scrumptious meals and ensure that your river rafting adventure will leave you feeling that Africa’s magic has touched your soul. Come and experience a truly remarkable special canoe orange river rafting excursion with us!

Come run with us

Apart from our wonderful rafting sessions we also have a wonderful Sunset Cabins. Its on the top section of the A-frame wooden house. Directly below Sunset Cabin is Seekaroo Dormitory. A booking can only be done to an individual group. We have 8 canvas tents that we equip with mattresses and fitted sheets.Each tent is pitched near the river and sleeping in one of them provides the ultimate experience when visiting us for your river rafting excursion. Please bring along your own bedding, towels and a torch.
