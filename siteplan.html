Raftalious

Raftalious - The destess pill

Site Purpose and Goals

The website will be to market the Raftalious rafting activities to the world especially those who live around it. The Site will also
allow the customers to make payments online before they even visit. It will also give a full description of all the activities
that cane be done. Raftalious provides rafting services to individuals, groups and even organisations who might decide to come for some
team building activities.

Target Audience

The target audience will be anyone who is able bodied between the ages of 16 to 65years and financially sustainable. The site
will be accessible on both computers and mobile devices.
