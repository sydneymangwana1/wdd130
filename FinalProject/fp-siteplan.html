Rent-A-Firewall

Rent-A-Firewall - Cyber Secure 24/7

Site Purpose and Goals

Have you ever imagined how vulnerable the ordinary non-security technical people are when connected to the internet? What about the cost and knowledge required for one to be secure when online? Imagine we have a website that rents out such devices and services at a very limited cost! We completely take away the management and maintenance of the security device and services from the ordinary users or business owners as we place the burden solely upon ourselves for a small monthly cost. Almost all service providers in the country sell such devices but with us we rent them out and this will take away the big “$” sign from the ordinary people’s mind opening an avenue for them to start seeing the affordability of such devices and services.

Target Audience

My Target audience will be the small to mdeium companys that relay on the internet for their day to day work. This also includes house holds that are constantly connected to the internet
